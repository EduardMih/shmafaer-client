The platform aims to help with projects management inside faculty. It makes it easier for students and professors to send their projects to Software Heritage, download archived projects, view and search for projects and get recommendations of popular GitHub repositories based on their search interests.
Manage projects manage

Students and professors can view and search all projects and edit theirs projects.

 Rate projects star

Professors and Students can rate projects according to their preferences.

 Archive archive

Professors can chose what projects they want to send to Software Heritage for archiving.

 Download download

Professors and students can download projects that were archived to Software Heritage.
